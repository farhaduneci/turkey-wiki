# Document Translation: Original vs. Copy

When registering at a Turkish university, your diploma and transcript need to be translated by a certified translator. Finding one isn't the hard part.

## The Mistake

Translators often ask you to send a photo or scan of your document instead of meeting in person. The problem is what ends up written inside the translation — if they state the translation was done from a **copy** of the document rather than the **original**, you'll run into serious problems down the line.

If you have the original document, hand it over in person and explicitly ask the translator to note that the translation was made from the original, not a copy.

## Notarization

Every translated document in Turkey must then be notarized at a **noter** (notary office). This costs around **1,700 TRY per page** of translation.
