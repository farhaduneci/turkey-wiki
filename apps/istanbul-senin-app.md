# İstanbul Senin App

🪪 **Requires a Turkish ID / foreigner ID number (Yabancı Kimlik Numarası) for its main features** — see below.

"İstanbul Senin" ("Istanbul is Yours") is the official mobile app from the Istanbul Metropolitan Municipality (İBB — İstanbul Büyükşehir Belediyesi). It's a single gateway to a lot of city services that used to be scattered across separate apps and websites — WiFi sign-up is just one small piece of it, not the main point.

## What it's used for

- Applying for the **İBB Kart** discounts and other transit-related benefits, including the discounted/student Istanbulkart application — see [İstanbulkart App](istanbulkart-app.md)
- Signing up for **free public WiFi** hotspots around the city (see [Free WiFi in Istanbul](../internet/free-wifi.md))
- Reporting municipal issues (broken infrastructure, complaints) through a "White Desk" (Beyaz Masa) style feature
- Checking announcements, events, and cultural activities run by the municipality (concerts, museum passes, etc.)
- Accessing some municipal payments (e.g. water bill, certain fines) depending on your district

## Signing up

- Available on both the App Store and Google Play — search "İstanbul Senin"
- Registration typically asks for your phone number (Turkish number) and an SMS verification code
- **The Kart discount and benefit applications require a Turkish ID/foreigner ID number (Yabancı Kimlik Numarası)** — meaning you'll need an active residence permit application before those features work, not just a Turkish SIM. Basic WiFi sign-up doesn't need it.
- The interface is in Turkish by default; some sections have partial English support depending on app version

> Note: app features, required documents, and available languages change fairly often as İBB updates the app — verify current requirements in-app before relying on this for something time-sensitive.
