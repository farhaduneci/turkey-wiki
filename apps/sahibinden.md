# Sahibinden

Sahibinden ("from the owner" in Turkish) is the leading online classifieds and shopping platform in Türkiye, where individuals and businesses buy, sell, and rent everything from real estate and vehicles to second-hand goods — connecting people directly rather than through a middleman.

## What it's used for

- **Finding a house or apartment** to rent or buy — this is the main platform most people use for housing search
- Buying and selling vehicles
- Buying and selling second-hand goods of pretty much any kind (furniture, electronics, appliances, etc.)

It's a must-have app if you're apartment hunting or furnishing a place — far more listings than any other platform in Turkey.

## Signing up

You can open an account with just a phone number — no Turkish ID/foreigner ID number required to browse or create a basic account.
