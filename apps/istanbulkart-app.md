# İstanbulkart App

🪪 **Requires a Turkish ID / foreigner ID number (Yabancı Kimlik Numarası) to apply for the discounted/student card.**

Separate from the physical [Istanbulkart](../transportation/istanbulkart.md) card itself, İBB also runs a mobile app for managing it — checking balance, transfer history, and (the part most foreigners care about) applying for the discounted **student Istanbulkart** instead of paying full/tourist fares.

## Applying for the Student Card

Once you have an **open residence permit application** (you don't need the permit itself yet, just an active application in the system) and your student status is registered, you become eligible to apply for the discounted student card — see [İstanbul Senin App](istanbul-senin-app.md), which is where the Kart application itself is submitted.

After approval, the physical card isn't handed to you in person — it's mailed to the address you registered, via **PTT** (the Turkish postal service).

> **Mistake I made:** I wasn't home when PTT tried to deliver mine, and getting it back was harder than it should have been. I went to the nearest PTT office; they told me the package would be brought there in 2 days — it wasn't. Eventually a PTT officer just called me out of nowhere and said he'd leave it at the address. It worked out, but only by chance. **Make sure you (or someone who can sign for it) will actually be at the registered address when the card is expected**, or arrange to have it held at your local PTT office in advance if you can. Having a Turkish-speaking friend who can call PTT on your behalf makes this a lot less stressful — worth asking one for help if you have the option.
