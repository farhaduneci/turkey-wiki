A collection of everything I've learned while living in Turkey.

Instead of letting useful information disappear in chats, bookmarks, and notes, I'm documenting it here—for myself and anyone else who might find it helpful.

* 🎓 Student life
* 🏠 Housing

* 💰 Costs & budgeting
* 💡 Tips & recommendations
* ⚠️ [Mistakes I made (so you don't have to)](mistakes/)

## Why?

Moving to another country comes with a lot of questions, and the answers are often scattered across forums, Reddit posts, Telegram groups, and official websites.

This repository is my attempt to collect everything in one place—based on my experiences.
